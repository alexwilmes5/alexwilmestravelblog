Puerto Rico is an island located in the Carribean Sea. It is a territory of the United States of America, and great travel destination for anyone who wants to enjoy beautiful weather and have an awesome experience in the Carribean. I have been to Puerto Rico's capital San Juan, 4 times, and Puerto Rico remains one of my favorite places in the world. Because Puerto Rico is a territory of the U.S., you don't need a passport to travel there. If you would like to learn more about traveling to Puerto Rico, click here.
